You know that the best way to stay one step ahead is to keep tabs on the competition.

Wiretap activates after completing a total of 33% worth of repair progress on generators.

After repairing a Generator for at least 3 seconds, press the Active Ability Button 1 to install a Wiretap, which stays active for 60/70/80 seconds. The Aura of trapped generators is revealed in yellow to all Survivors.

  • When the Killer enters within 14 meters of the trapped generator, their aura is revealed to all Survivors.
  • Wiretrap deactivates when the trapped generator is damaged by the Killer or when the timer expires.
